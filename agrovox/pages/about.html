AgroVox Smart Probe

AI-Assisted Soil Analysis & Precision Farming Device

M. Kumarasamy College of Engineering · CSBS Dept · 2025–2026

📋 Abstract

Agriculture is an important sector supporting livelihoods in rural areas. Traditional soil testing methods are slow and rely on laboratory analysis, delaying decision-making. AgroVox Smart Probe is an intelligent soil analysis system providing real-time field-level insights with simple voice-based decision support — accessible even without smartphones or internet.

🔬 Evaluation Parameters

💧
Soil Moisture
Optimal: 30–70%
Measures water content in soil. Below 30% triggers irrigation. Above 70% signals over-watering.
🌡️
Temperature
Optimal: 15–35°C
Monitors soil thermal conditions. High temps above 35°C require increased watering frequency.
⚗️
pH Level
Optimal: 6.0–8.0
Acidic soils (pH<6) need lime. Alkaline soils (pH>8) need organic compost to balance.
⚡
Electrical Conductivity
Optimal: ≥1.0 dS/m
Estimates salinity and nutrient levels. Low EC indicates nutrient deficiency requiring fertilization.

📈 Moving Average Filter

Formula
MA(t) = [x(t) + x(t-1) + x(t-2) + x(t-3) + x(t-4)] / 5

The system uses a 5-point moving average filter to eliminate sensor noise and environmental fluctuations. This ensures stable readings are fed into the decision engine, minimizing false alerts and improving recommendation accuracy. The filter operates in real-time on the ESP32 microcontroller with minimal processing overhead.
